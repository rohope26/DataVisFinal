How to Use This Site

This site explores where objects in The Met collection are recorded as coming from, using open access collection data to make the museum's global reach easier to see.

  • Use the period dropdown to filter the map by time period.
  • Hover over a country to see its artifact count.
  • Click a country to view example artifacts and a timeline for that country.
  • Open the U.S. vs World page to compare United States objects with another selected country and the rest of the world.
