How to Use This Site

This site explores where objects in The Met collection are recorded as coming from, using open access collection data to make the museum's global reach easier to see.

  • On this page, choose a country to compare it with the United States and the rest of the world.
  • The bar labels show both artifact counts and each group's share of the loaded dataset.
  • Use the Map View page to explore countries geographically, filter by period, and click a country for artifacts and a timeline.
